Once a subscription plan is initiated and the agreement is signed, no refunds will be issued for the subscription period that has already been paid. This policy is in place to uphold the stability and reliability of our services.

FleetBox subscription plans are structured for long-term commitments, fostering a strong and stable partnership. Early termination of the agreement will not warrant any refund.

In the event of premature termination of the agreement, a penalty will be applied. This penalty is designed to cover the costs associated with early termination and includes the forfeiture of the security deposit.

FleetBox recognizes that unforeseen circumstances may arise. In exceptional cases, such as force majeure events or circumstances beyond the control of the subscriber, the company may consider exceptions to this policy. Any such consideration will be made at the sole discretion of FleetBox.
